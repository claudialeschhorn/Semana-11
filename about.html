Daniel Leschhorn

  • Birth Name: Daniel Leschhorn Manosalvas

  • Born: April 15th, 2004. Santo Domingo Dominican Republic

  • Genres: Indie Rock, Psychedelic Rock, Lo-Fi


Biography

Daniel Leschhorn Manosalvas (April 15, 2004) is a Dominican singer-songwriter, multi-instrumentalist and producer. Leschhorn has released 1 EP, called Engrudo Especial (2018). His style of music has been described as "blue wave" and "slacker rock","jizz jazz".
